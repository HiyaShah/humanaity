About Us

We are a group of high school students from Amador Valley High School passionate about teaching artificial intelligence concepts and teaching students how to build their own coding projects regardless of experience level. At our high school, we teach weekly workshops to ~30 students covering artificial intelligence concepts like Naive Bayes, Support Vector Machines, Random Forest, Reinforcement Learning, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, and Generative AI.

Our Artificial Intelligence workshop is a free, joint initiative in partnership with UC Berkeley Artificial Intelligence researchers to teach students across the U.S. to learn to code real artificial intelligence coding projects - no prior experience needed! We hope to see you at our workshop!
